Unravel Design System

A comprehensive brand template showcasing the chalkboard-inspired design system that brings interactive STEM learning to life through thoughtful visual design.

Brand Overview

Our design system is inspired by the classic chalkboard aesthetic, creating a warm, educational environment that makes learning feel approachable and engaging.

Visual Identity

Chalkboard-inspired design with chalk text effects, subtle textures, and educational iconography.

Typography

Color Usage Guidelines

Primary Usage

Chalk White: Main text, headings, primary content
Chalk Yellow: Brand accent, highlights, primary actions
Board Dark/Darker: Background layers, containers

Subject-Specific Usage

Use subject colors for icons, badges, and section headers
Apply glow effects for enhanced visibility
Maintain contrast ratios for accessibility

Design Guidelines

Best Practices

Use chalk text effects for all primary content
Maintain consistent subject color associations
Apply glass morphism effects to cards and containers
Include subtle texture overlays for chalkboard feel
Use handwritten fonts for headings, clean fonts for body

Things to Avoid

Don't use bright white backgrounds
Avoid mixing subject colors randomly
Don't remove the chalkboard texture
Avoid overly bright or neon colors
Don't use inconsistent typography
